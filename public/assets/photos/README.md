## Preview (local dev)

Open **http://localhost:4321/photos-preview/** to browse engagement photos and current slot assignments.

**Favorites** (saved in your browser via localStorage):

- **♡ / ♥** on each thumbnail toggles favorite (click the heart, not the image)
- **Click the image** to open zoom view for comparing shots
- In zoom: **← →** prev/next, **Esc** close, **F** favorite
- Favorites appear in the top bar and at the top of the grid
- Drag chips to reorder; drag a thumbnail into the dashed area to favorite
- **×** on a chip removes a favorite
- **Copy filenames** exports your ordered favorite list
